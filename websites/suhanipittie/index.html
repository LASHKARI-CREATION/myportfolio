about

SUHANI PITTIE

Enlisted by the World Gold Council as the ' top 10 most inventive and ingenious jewellery designers in the world', this graduate from the Gemological Institute of America established her own training institute at the young age of twenty and is most recently a recipient of the Woman Entrepreneur of the Year award by the Retail Guild, India. 

Credited by Elle magazine for changing India's perspective towards precious jewellery, Suhani Pittie has featured in international magazines like Vogue Italia as one of the '5 designers to watch out for from India'. Back home, her creations have been featured in magazines like Vogue, Harpers Bazaar, Grazia, Femina to name a few.
